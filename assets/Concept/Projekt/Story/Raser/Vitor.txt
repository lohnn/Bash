Vitor is the youngest and the foulest of the three. Lacking strength and cunning, Vitor rely on trickery and betrayal. This have caused him to be revered
as the god of thieves. Vitor is also the only god that can be contacted. Priests of Vitor can sacrifice animals and humans to get a brief conversation with
Vitor. This have caused Vitor to be a popular god, and is worshipped in temple's all over the world. However Vitor's inteference with the world have caused 
his other brothers to lose respect for him. Vitor is a selfish god, that will use and abuse people as much as he like to get what he want.
